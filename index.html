Privacy Policy for Church Audio Hub

Last Updated: April 11, 2025

Welcome to Church Audio Hub, an app developed by Demakk ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our app to stream church sermons, music, and related content. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use Church Audio Hub:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and name to manage your account and provide personalized features like playlists and listening history.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, such as the sermons or music you listen to, your playlists, and your offline downloads.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information, such as your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, to optimize app performance.
  • Analytics Data: We use third-party analytics tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics) to collect anonymized data about app usage, such as the number of users, session duration, and crash reports, to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app’s functionality, such as streaming audio content and saving your playlists.
  • To personalize your experience, such as recommending sermons or music based on your listening history.
  • To send notifications about new sermons, music, or app updates (you can opt out of these in the app settings).
  • To analyze app usage and improve our services, such as fixing bugs or adding new features.
  • To comply with legal obligations, such as retaining financial records if you make donations through the app.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services like Firebase (for authentication, analytics, and notifications) that may process your data on our behalf. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a court order or to protect the safety of our users.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with other parties if you give us explicit permission to do so.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes using encryption for data transmission and secure storage for your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Account and Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time. To do so:

  • Use the "Delete Account" option in the app’s settings, or
  • Visit our Account Deletion Request page to submit a request.

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your account and associated data, including your profile, playlists, and listening history, within 7 days. We may retain some data for legal purposes, such as financial records related to donations, for a period of 7 years as required by tax regulations in Ethiopia. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data, as described above.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of notifications or analytics tracking (via app settings).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@yourchurchwebsite.com.

7. Third-Party Services

Church Audio Hub uses third-party services like Firebase for authentication, analytics, and notifications. These services may collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Firebase’s Privacy Policy for more information: Firebase Privacy Policy.

8. Children’s Privacy

Church Audio Hub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Please review this policy periodically.
